Veterinarian Finder

This veterinarian finder application helps users find a veterinarian easily for their pets called VetFinder! This app integrates Google API and their maps tools to show locations of local Veterinarians. You can book appointments, view a list of your favourite veterinarians, and save your pets within the app.

What I Learned from this Project

Participating in a group project to develop a mobile application gaves me a learning experience involving both the technical and interpersonal skills. You get experience with using important tools and technologies like version control, integrated development envrionments, and mobile develop frameworks like Flutter. I learned how to split an entire complex mobile application into small, manageable components while developing user interfaces and backend functionality. Debuggin and testing was also a significant process as identifying and resolving issues effectively and efficiently was key to completing the project on time. This experience will help me greatly in real-world software development processes, preparing me for the pressional roles to come.

I also practiced my collaboration, communication, and general management from this group project. Our teamwork involved dividing tasks evenly, setting deadlines, and keeping everyone aligned with the project goals. Working on this project improved my collaboration in terms of solving problems together to ensure that the final product was functional. I also accommodated to changes in the needs of the build and communicated where necessary. These soft skills are equally as important and are essential for the success in future professional environments.
