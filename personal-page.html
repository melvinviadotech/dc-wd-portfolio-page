Background

The picture above describes my life right now. I wake up and drink coffee to get myself prepared for the work that needs to be done. I find programming enjoyable, I like challenges, and learning is a constant must for me. I find it interesting the different environment that I find myself in throughout these courses and assignments. Each lab or assignment is unique and brings new inqueries each time.

I spent most of my life either on a gaming console or on a computer. I found myself practicing computer programming because I was always surrounded with computer programs my entire life. I thought that it would probably be beneficial if I learned how to use computers to my advantage and I can support that statement now. I feel like a lot of auotmation can be made with computer as information is incredibly prevalent to this day. Sometimes I find myself programming even outside of College. I can use applications I create however I want and find joy in the process.

Interests

I also played sports throughout my life. I tried Soccer, Basketball, Badminton, Volleyball, and many more. Now, I enjoy a new and upcoming sport called Pickleball that is popular in the US. People hate on it because its basically a copy of Tennis, but I find it more fun and I think people should usually do what they enjoy if it does not come at the expense of other people.

At this point in my life, I have the most understanding of strategy mechanics of every sports and know what it takes to win. I am a firm believer in consistency, within evreything that you do in life, and it is so important to implement in it your daily life. Consistency in computer programming means sacrificing most of your time reading documentation, providing implmentation of new features to applications, and being patient with developing your skill as a developer. This is exactly what I strive to be and what I am working towards constantly. For some reason Pickleball really was my stepping stone for realizing this because many Pickleball videos said that the high level players have consistency and low level players do not.
